memes were originally conceptualized as units of cultural transfer, coined by richard dawkins in 1976, but the internet gave it an entirely different meaning. internet memes are cultural ideas that have been reinvented by human creativity, a contagious idea. humans have a tendency of being a part of something bigger than themselves. memes are a reflection of your cleverness, intellect, and worldly knowledge. what i find fascinating about memes is that people feel freer to flirt with the taboos due to the online disinhibition effect, coined by john suler. there's no social consequences in making memes, but memes help get statements across, conversations started.

for this project, i've created two different kinds of meme generators. personal/general. the personal meme generator, is obviously a gateway into the way i think, while crossing the lines between oversharing and what's generally socially acceptable to share. i find being brutally honest so relieving.

the general meme generator kind of works as a critique on the 21st century in the most vaguest sense, through dark humor of course. got to make it digestable somehow!

the site is set up like the theatre, as if we all have a performed social self. as much as we believe we are rugged individuals, releasing our will out into the world we are exisential beings, animating the multitude of selves ready made by society to fill the social roles in our lives in the context of a captialist society.
